My Journey

Built through dedication, growth, and love for the craft.

From the moment I attended my first lash training, I knew this was the path I wanted to follow — with passion, dedication, and precision.

From then on, I focused on improving every single day.

After completing my initial training, I began working in a beauty salon in my hometown, where I built my experience, refined my technique, and developed strong relationships with my clients.

Over time, this helped me gain confidence in my work and shape my own recognizable style.

As I grew, I felt the need to expand beyond my local environment. I started seeking new education and opportunities, allowing me to refine my skills and stay aligned with the latest trends in the lash industry.

Alongside my work with clients, I continue to learn through ongoing trainings and guidance from my mentor, who has supported me from the very beginning.

I believe the beauty industry is constantly evolving — and that true growth comes from continuous learning.

Services

Looks designed to enhance every style and mood.

01

Classic Lashes

A timeless, elegant look created by applying one extension to one natural lash. Perfect for clients who love softness, definition, and natural beauty.

02

Light Volume

A delicate step above classic lashes, adding airy fullness while keeping the overall result soft and balanced.

03

Volume

Designed for fuller, fluffier lash sets with more intensity and a beautifully textured finish.

04

Mega Volume

A bold, glamorous set for clients who want maximum density, depth, and drama in their lash look.

05

Drama Look

A more expressive, statement-making style tailored for clients who want their eyes to stand out instantly.

06

Lash Lift

A natural enhancement that lifts and defines your own lashes, creating an effortless and polished appearance.

07

HD Brows

Brow shaping and styling that frames the face beautifully and completes the overall look with softness and structure.
